How to find us

The Renaissance Rooms, is just a short walk from Vauxhall Station.


Parking nearby is available. Entrance is Via Wandsworth Road

Arrival by Underground

* Take exit 2 at Vauxhall Underground Station.
* Turn right at the top of the stairs and walk through bus station down to stop J
* Cross Parry St at pedestrian controlled lights.
* Turn right after crossing Parry street follow the wall round into Wandsworth Road.
* Follow the road down to Railway Bridge then turn left into the entrance to Roller Disco.

Bus

Daytime: 2, 89, 36, 185, 436, 360
Nighttime: N2, N36
